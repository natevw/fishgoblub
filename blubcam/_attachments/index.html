Behind-the-scenes

We're installing twenty Itek 240W solar photovoltaic modules to feed a grid-tied Eltek THEIA 4.4 inverter. This equipment is Washington manufactured, qualifying us for some lucrative state subsidies in addition to the federal tax rebate.

The overhead rig is based around Public Lab's Ballon Mapping Kit. I'm using an older Eye-Fi card to wirelessly "tether" my camera to some software I assembled for my Raspberry Pi. The pictures are then streamed to this site, which is hosted by IrisCouch.
